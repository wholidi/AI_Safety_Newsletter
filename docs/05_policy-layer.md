# 📜 Policy Layer

The policy layer injects governance rules into the pipeline.

---

## Internal Policy

- no personal data in prompts or outputs
- human review is mandatory
- no confidential data exposure
- do not copy AI output blindly

---

## Governance Policy

- no exaggerated compliance claims
- clearly separate fact vs interpretation
- include caveats and limitations
- ensure transparency and explainability

---

## Privacy Policy

- lawful data processing
- data minimization
- transparency of usage
- respect data subject rights

---

## Security Policy

- protect sensitive data
- enforce access control
- follow secure practices
- report incidents

---

## Accountability

- maintain audit logs
- record prompts, outputs, decisions
- ensure traceability across pipeline

---

## Design Principle

Policies are:

> **not static documents — but executable constraints applied at runtime**
