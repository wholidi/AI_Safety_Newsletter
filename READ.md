# 🧠 Urielle-AI — AI Safety Newsletter Pipeline

A multi-agent AI system that generates **AI Safety & Governance newsletters** with built-in:

- privacy policy enforcement  
- guardrails  
- verification layer  
- audit-ready outputs  

---

## 🔍 What This System Does

This pipeline transforms raw news into:

> **policy-aligned, risk-reviewed, and verifiable AI-generated content**

---

## 🚨 Why This Matters

AI-generated content often lacks:

- privacy compliance  
- governance alignment  
- traceability  
- audit evidence  

This creates risk in:
- regulatory communication  
- enterprise usage  
- compliance reporting  

---

## ✅ What This System Demonstrates

This system demonstrate how AI-generated content can be:

- enforce **privacy & policy at runtime**
- detect **overclaiming and unsafe outputs**
- convert outputs into **audit evidence**
- ensure **human-in-the-loop decision control
- constrained by policy
- verified at each stage
- traceable for audit
- prepared for human decision-making

---

## 🔄 Workflow Overview

RSS Input  
→ Agent 0 (News Scout)  
→ Guardrail + Verification  
→ Agent 1 (Draft Writer)  
→ Guardrail + Verification  
→ Agent 2 (Governance Reviewer)  
→ Decision Engine (Approve / Revise / Reject)  
→ Agent 3 (Revision)  
→ Final Guardrail + Verification  
→ Manual Review Pack (HTML)

---

## 📊 Output

Example output:

- Manual Review Pack (HTML)
  - risk score & severity
  - governance issues
  - policy gaps
  - recommended edits
  - audit metadata
  - human decision section

---

## 🧩 Architecture Layers

- **Agent Layer** → content generation & evaluation
- **Guardrail Layer** → structural & safety validation
- **Policy Layer** → governance rules injection
- **Verification Layer** → audit evidence generation
- **Human Layer** → final decision
